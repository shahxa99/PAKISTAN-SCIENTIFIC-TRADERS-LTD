HISTORY

Back in 1987, all thanks to the vision of Mr. Ali Ahmad Athar, he found a remarkable company Pakistan Scientific Traders came into existence. Initially, our focus was on providing repair and backup services to Intermediate, Graduate Level, and Medical Colleges.

Over the course of three challenging decades, Mr. Ali Ahmad Athar dedicated himself tirelessly to the company's growth. Through his unwavering commitment, Pakistan Scientific Traders earned its place as a respected and well-known company in the business world.
