HISTORY

Pakistan Scientific Traders were founded in 1987 by Mr. Ali Ahmad Athar. Initially started the business by providing repair and backup services to Intermediate, Graduate Level and Medical Colleges. After a continuous struggle of three decades by Mr. Ali Ahmad Athar, it made Pakistan Scientific Traders as well-known company.
